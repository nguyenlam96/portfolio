About Continua

Continua is an iOS app designed to empower patients by helping them record their health conditions and book appointments with practitioners through online meetings. With features like symptom tracking, profile management, and seamless video consultations, Continua makes healthcare accessible and efficient. Built with Swift for a smooth user experience.

Screenshots

Login Screen

The login screen welcomes users back to Continua, offering a secure entry point with email and password fields. Users can also register or recover their password with ease.

Home Screen with Upcoming Appointment

The home screen provides a quick overview of upcoming appointments, health tracking options like surveys and food journals, and easy access to assistance and adherence tools.

Practitioner Selection Screen

Users can browse a list of available practitioners, view their specialties, locations, and recent activity, making it easy to find the right doctor for their needs.

Doctor Profile and Scheduling Screen

This screen displays a doctor's profile, including their credentials and availability. Users can select a date, duration, and time slot to schedule an appointment.

Appointment Booking Screen

Users can confirm their appointment details, select the appointment type (e.g., video), and specify the reason for the visit before booking.

Payment Screen

The payment screen allows users to review the appointment cost, select a payment method, and securely complete the transaction to confirm their booking.

User Profile Screen

The user profile screen lets patients manage their personal information, including contact details, address, and documents like passports or driver's licenses.

Video Consultation Screen

This screen enables seamless video consultations with practitioners, offering controls for muting, ending the call, and accessing chat features for a smooth experience.

Health Condition Recording Screen

Patients can record their health conditions by selecting affected areas on a body map and rating the severity of symptoms and stress levels for better tracking.

Head Condition Assessment Screen

This detailed assessment screen allows users to specify head-related symptoms, such as pain or itching, and rate their severity, with the option to upload photos for documentation.

Chest Condition Assessment Screen

Users can document chest-related symptoms like heart flutter or chest pain, specify severity, and add detailed notes to share with their practitioner.

Adding Payment Method Screen

This screen allows users to add a new payment method by entering card details manually or scanning their card, ensuring a secure and convenient payment setup for appointments.
